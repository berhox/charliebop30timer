CHOOSE YOUR ALLEGIANCE

THE BEEZIANS (The Ancients)

The oldest and most powerful entities in Jondique. They trace their lineage back to the creation of the server itself. While other factions squabble over land, the Beezians hold knowledge of the "Before Times." Their bases are monuments to excess and power, often paved in gold blocks. They are the closest kin to the owner, Rybeez, and view the current war as mere entertainment.

ARUNDEL (DARRWEN'S DOMAIN)

Arundel was once the picture of suburban perfection, known for its pristine lawns and elegant builds. That dream is dead. **Darrwen**, a ruthless gang warlord, arrived and seized the territory. Now, the manicured lawns hide a dark secret. Arundel has become the capital of the black market—a **drug and weapon empire** where illicit potions are brewed in hidden basements and illegal enchantments are forged in backyards. It is a beautiful facade covering a rot of crime.

CARRARA (The Iron Enclave)

A sprawling industrial complex of stone and iron. They are the undisputed masters of trade, redstone machinery, and enchanting. In Carrara, everything has a price, and loyalty is bought with diamonds. They view Arundel as dangerous competition and the **Hoxians as overrated**.

THE BOPIANS (The Slow Hands)

The most peaceful faction, but perhaps the most frustrating. The Bopians are legendary for their builds, but they move at a glacial pace. A Bopian might take three months to finish a roof, but that roof will be the most beautiful thing you have ever seen. They generally stay out of the war, preferring to sit in boats and stare at blocks for hours.

HOXIANS (THE WINNER'S ARCANA)

The **Hoxians** are the undisputed masters of non-combative excellence. They are the server's **second most powerful** collective, achieving dominance not through PVP, but through sheer talent and ability in architecture, Redstone engineering, and economic strategy. Their leader, **Berhox**, is known across Jondique simply as "**The Winner**"—a title earned through a series of tactical victories and unknown maneuvers that always leave his enemies utterly defeated.

KURAIANS (THE BEDRIDDEN DIPLOMATS)

The **Kuraians** have fully embraced sloth and are undeniably the laziest faction on the server. They operate from deep, comfortable bases only to avoid the hassle of the surface. Their leader, **Kurai**, is a cat obsessed with maximizing comfort, demanding a steady supply of frappes and large fries. Their entire strategic contribution relies solely on their powerful, yet exhausted, ally **Dxvo**, who grudgingly carries the entire faction's weight.
